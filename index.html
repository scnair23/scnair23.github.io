Sivasankar Nair

I recently obtained my Ph.D. from the Département de Mathématiques et de Statistique of the Université de Montréal. My supervisor was Prof. Matilde Lalín, and I am interested in special values of L-functions and Mahler measures. I was one of the many people working in Number Theory in the vibrant city of Montréal! I was previously a Master's student at UBC, supervised by Prof. Sujatha Ramdorai and Prof. Vinayak Vatsal, and an undergraduate student at IIT Kanpur.
